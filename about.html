About Us

Established in 2011, we started with a simple aim; to provide Sherwood with great coffee!

Sherwood is a brilliant and welcoming area. It's brimming with creativity and vibrancy. We thought it would be an excellent area to open our coffee house in.

Our aim is to be the most friendly and inclusive coffee house in the local area. We also really care about sustainability so we are constantly adapting our store.
